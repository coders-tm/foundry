# Support Tickets

## Summary
Expertise in support ticket lifecycle management including ticket creation, replies, status transitions, assignment, and customer support workflows.

## Key Domains
- Ticket creation and status tracking
- Reply management and conversation threads
- Status state machine (open, in-progress, awaiting-response, resolved, closed)
- Ticket assignment and routing
- Priority and category management
- SLA and response time tracking
- Ticket events and notifications

## When to Activate
- Creating or updating support tickets
- Adding replies to ticket conversations
- Managing ticket status transitions
- Assigning tickets to support agents
- Querying support ticket queues
- Ticket search and filtering
